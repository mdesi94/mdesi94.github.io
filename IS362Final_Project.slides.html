Tracking the 2019 NBA Draft Lottery Picks: Most Improved Rookie

Goal: Determine the most improved player from their rookie to sophmore seasons based on total points, assists, 2-point%, 3-point%, and steals.

Notes: This project will only take into account the lottery picks (first 10 players drafted) of the 2019 draft.

Part 3: Conclusions

The goal of this project was to determine the most improved player based only on average points per game (PTS), assists (AST), 3-point percentage (3P%), 2-point percentage (2P%), and steals (STL).

It is clear to see who improved most in each category by viewing the bar plots above. Since Darius Garland had the most improvement in 3/5 categories, he is the winner!

There are many other stats and factors that contribute to the actual improvement of players. For example, minutes per game and games played can impact these statistics drastically, but were not considered in this example.
